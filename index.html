The Artist

Kasmira Seymour

Visual Artist & Creative Strategist · Watercolor · Poetry · Workshops

Visual artist, poet, and educator creating work rooted in healing, resilience, and light. Based in Wilmington, Delaware. Available for art licensing, commissions, workshops, and brand partnerships.

The Art+

Working across watercolor, acrylic, oil painting, and clay, each body of work unfolds from a sustained inquiry into healing, identity, and what it means to be fully human, returning to self.

The Practice+

Original prints available as instant digital downloads. Original paintings sold by request only.
